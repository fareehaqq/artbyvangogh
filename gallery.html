PAINTER; POST-IMPRESSIONIST

Vincent Van Gogh is a Dutch artist based in Amsterdam.

His bold and distinct style is characterized by the use of vibrant colours and expressive, dynamic brushwork.

Van Gogh's unique style has established him as one of the most influential artists in the modern art scene today. His work is considered to have laid the foundation for modern art.
